What the PYP Exhibition is about

The PYP Exhibition is a culminating project for students in the Primary Years Programme (PYP) of the International Baccalaureate (IB). It is an opportunity for students to showcase their learning and understanding of a specific topic or issue. The exhibition is typically held at the end of the PYP program, and it allows students to demonstrate their skills in research, communication, and critical thinking.

During the exhibition, students work collaboratively in groups to investigate a topic of their choice. They conduct research, create presentations, and engage with the community to share their findings. The exhibition is a celebration of student learning and provides an opportunity for students to reflect on their growth and development throughout the PYP program.
